Soft Tacos

Description

These soft tacos will have you reaching for more. Extremely popular they pair well with ricardo chicken and rice.

Ingredients

  • 4 Cups Flower
  • 1/2 Cup Butter
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/4 Cups Water
  • 1 tsp baking powder

Instructions

  1. Mix all the dry ingredients
  2. Soften butter but don't melt it.
  3. Mix all the dry ingredients and butter
  4. Add water
  5. Heat a cast iron pan and cook the tortillas on each side until nicely browned
